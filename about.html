About

Kaila Allen is a UI/UX designer based in Honolulu, Hawaii. Originally from Maryland, Kaila moved to Hawaii with her family when she was 4 years old and has lived there since.

From her childhood, Kaila showed a natural talent for the arts and other creative endeavors, a passion that was encouraged by her teachers, friends, and family alike. As a child, she enjoyed dancing hula (a traditional Hawaiian form of dance), as well as ballet, drawing, painting, and other creative outlets.

This passion for the arts continues on to this day as Kaila enjoys oil painting and photography which she uses to both express herself and connect with others. Her love for art and design combined with her education in computer science would eventually lead Kaila to her current career in UI/UX. While studying at the University of Hawaii, she worked in a data visualization lab and contributed to various projects related to artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and augmented reality, an experience that furthered her understanding and interest in the tech industry. After graduating, Kaila would go on to become a junior UI/UX designer, eventually leading teams on various design projects.

For those looking to work in the UI/UX design field, Kaila recommends creating a portfolio website that showcases both personal and professional projects to get a leg up in the job search. She also advises finding a passion for the work and a genuine interest in solving problems by understanding users thoughts and emotions while balancing design and functionality.

Kaila takes pride in being a kind and loving presence for the people she cares about and cites her resilient mother as being her biggest influence in life.
